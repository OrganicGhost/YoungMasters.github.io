About Our School

Young Masters is an award-winning visual arts school for children mainly between four and 16 years old that runs on a weekly basis in two terms

Both terms end with a free public exhibition of the students’ work at various locations in Bradford and Leeds.

At Young Masters we aim to teach children painting and drawing techniques, sculpture techniques, and art history. We are based in Shipley, in close proximity to the Leeds-Liverpool canal and Saltaire Village.

Young Masters is the ideal place for young children to discover and develop their natural talent and skills. We provide Level 1 pre-GCSE (age 7 and above) and Level 2 GCSE (age 11 and above) qualifications.

Visual Art School Classes

Young Masters Regular classes are mainly designed for school age Y3 - Y9. We build on a wide range of skills and techniques.

Our Regular classes cover Art history, observational drawing, sketching, shading, soft pastels - all the way through to painting techniques in watercolour and acrylics. We also cover 3D modelling, clay work and paper mache, along with printing techniques and much more.

Our classes are fun and informative, and we strive to get the most out of all our students- offering encouragement and support. We exhibit our work regularly and submit to competitions along the way. We start the term by looking at still life projects, but with a typical Young Masters dash of fun to accompany the classes.

Our Classes

Home Educated Classes

In our friendly home educated classes we introduce our students to a wide range of artists, illustrators and art techniques

Mini Masters Programme

We invite you to attend with your child, and work together to create beautiful artworks and learn new skills

Textiles Classes

At Young Masters we will be exploring a range of different decorative textile techniques from hand embroidery to free machine embroidery.

Photgraphy Classes

Students explore a variety of themes and genres and develop an eye for design and what makes a great photograph.

Illustration Classes

A chance to explore our imaginations and delve headlong into the beguiling World of Illustrations through history.

Master Painting Classes

Use a range of materials mainly watercolours, acrylic paints and oil paints and where appropriate we will be painting on canvas.
